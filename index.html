About the Workshop

Dive deep into the fascinating world of quantum computing with this comprehensive 3-day workshop. Learn from leading experts in quantum information theory, quantum security, and practical Qiskit programming.

This hands-on training program is designed to give you a solid foundation in quantum computing principles and practical experience with cutting-edge quantum programming tools.

Whether you're a student, researcher, or professional, this workshop will equip you with the knowledge and skills to start your journey in quantum computing.

Quantum Fundamentals

Master the core principles of quantum information theory and open quantum systems

Qiskit Programming

Hands-on sessions with IBM's Qiskit framework for quantum programming

Quantum Networks

Explore quantum security, cryptography, and quantum network applications
